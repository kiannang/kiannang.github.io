Welcome to my little corner of the internet. I'm Kianna, a PhD student at UC Merced working on computer vision, robotic perception, and human-centered AI. I spend most of my time thinking about how machines see the world and how we can make that safer and more useful for people.

## Why I'm writing

I've realized that a lot of what I learn doesn't really stick unless I write it down. Whether it's a paper I'm trying to understand, an idea I'm exploring, or just something that finally clicks, it’s easy for it to disappear if I don't take a moment to reflect on it.

This blog is my way of keeping track of that.

It's also a way to think more clearly. Writing forces me to slow down and actually understand something instead of just feeling like I do.

## What to expect

No strict schedule, no content strategy. Just posts when I have something worth saying.

That might include:

- Things I'm building or experimenting with  
- Concepts I'm trying to understand  
- Thoughts on papers or ideas  
- Occasional reflections on PhD life  
- Thoughts on shows, games, or anything else I've been into lately  

## A note on audience

I'm writing for a general technically curious reader, not necessarily someone in my exact subfield. If I can't explain something clearly enough for that, I probably don't understand it well enough myself.

## To start

Thanks for being here. More soon. <3

— Kianna
